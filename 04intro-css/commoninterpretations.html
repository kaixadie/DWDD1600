Common Interpretations

What are the most typical types of dreams that can be quickly interpreted?

Falling in a dream
Something going wrong in life, need to rethink a choice, consider a new direction
Public nudity
Feeling like a phony in real life, afraid of revealing imperfections and shortcomings
Being chased
Hiding anger, passions, other feelings, childhood experiences indicating past trauma, afraid of love or haunted by a past relationship
Losing some/all of your teeth
Worried about appearance, concerned about communication, shared an embarrassing thought
Dreaming of dying
Fear of change, mourning for passage of time, preparing for death
Taking a test
Feeling unprepared, especially for challenges in waking life
Infidelity
Not getting what you need from the relationship, fear of infidelity, what ifs of reality
Flying
Independence, sexuality, expressing freedoms
Pregnancy
Fears of inadequacy in parenthood, presage difficult times
